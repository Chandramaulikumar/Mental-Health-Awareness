WHO ARE WE?


  • Mental health is just as important as physical health and should be given equal attention and care..


  • Everyone can experience mental health challenges at some point in their life, and it is not a sign of weakness to seek help.


  • Mental illness is not something that can be "cured," but it can be managed with the right treatment and support.


  • Good mental health is not just the absence of mental illness, but also the ability to cope with the stresses of everyday life and maintain healthy relationships.


  • There are many different types of mental health conditions and no two people will experience them in the same way.


  • Everyone can take steps to improve their mental health and well-being, such as exercise, healthy eating, and stress management techniques.



    • OUR AIM


  • To educate the public about mental health and mental illness.


  • To reduce the stigma and discrimination associated with mental illness


  • To promote early identification and intervention for mental health issues


  • To increase access to mental health services and support


  • To empower individuals with mental illness to lead fulfilling lives


  • To advocate for policies and laws that support mental health and well-being


  • To promote research on mental health and mental illness.




    • OUR MISSION


  • Increase public awareness about mental health and mental illness


  • Reduce the stigma associated with mental illness


  • Encourage people to seek help when they need it, without shame or fear of judgment Provide support resources for people living with mental illness and their loved ones


  • Work with healthcare professionals, policymakers, and community organizations to improve access to mental health services


  • Promote mental health in the workplace and other community settings.
